# Early Stage — Can I Use Fairmint?

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

Yes — Fairmint is designed for companies at every stage, including early-stage startups. Many of our customers start using Fairmint from day one. Fairmint supports companies from pre-seed through Series C and beyond.

## What Early-Stage Companies Use Fairmint For

- **Raising a seed round** — create a SAFE series, share invitation links with investors, and collect signed agreements and payments
- **Managing equity grants** — issue stock options or RSUs to early employees with built-in vesting schedules
- **Building a cap table** — import your existing equity documents to build your cap table
- **Staying compliant** — KYC/KYB verification, accreditation checks, and AML screening are available as part of the investor onboarding process
