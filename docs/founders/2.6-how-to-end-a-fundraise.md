# How to End a Fundraise

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

When your fundraising campaign has reached its goals or the end date, you may need to officially close your fundraising round.

## Steps to End Your Fundraise

1. **Access Your Offering Page**: Log into your admin account and go to the offering page for the fundraise you want to end.
2. **Navigate to Advanced Management**: Scroll down to the very bottom of the page to find the "Advanced Management" dropdown menu.
3. **Close Your Fundraising Round**: Click on "Close Fundraising Round". This action will officially end the campaign and prevent any further investments.
