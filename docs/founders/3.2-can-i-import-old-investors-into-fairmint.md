# Can I Import Old Investors into Fairmint?

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

Integrating your existing investor data into Fairmint is a straightforward process. If you're looking to consolidate your previous investments and streamline your cap table management, here's how you can import old investors into your Fairmint account.

## Steps to Import Old Investors

Yes — use the cap table import feature. See [Import Your Cap Table](3.1-upload-import-cap-table.md) for the full walkthrough.

## Consolidating Prior Investors with Fairmint One

With Fairmint One, you have the option to further streamline your cap table by consolidating all previous investors into a single line item. This feature simplifies ongoing management and enhances clarity by providing a consolidated view of past investments. Please reach out to our team for more information.
