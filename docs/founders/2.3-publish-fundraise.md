# Publish a Fundraise

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

Publishing makes your deal page live so investors can view your terms and invest. Before publishing, you'll need to complete a checklist of requirements.

## Where to Find the Publish Button

After creating a fundraise, you land on the **deal page editor**. The **Publish** button is in the top-right corner of the editor, next to **View as an investor** and **Save & Close**.

You can also access the deal page editor later from your [Series](https://one.fairmint.com/p/{portalId}/series) page by clicking on a fundraise.

## The Publish Checklist

When you click **Publish**, you'll see a screen titled **"Ready to engage investors?"**. You must confirm all five items before the publish button activates:

1. **I have finalized my deal terms and completed my deal page** — review your terms and make sure your deal page content is ready
2. **I have updated details about my company and signer** — complete your company information and designate who will sign on behalf of the company
3. **I have added my bank details to receive funds from investors** — set up your bank account so you can receive wire transfers
4. **I have activated my custom domain to send investors to my website** — configure your DNS so investors visit a branded URL
5. **I have reviewed and tested the integration of my own legal agreement and confirmed it is correct** — verify your investment agreement works as expected

Each underlined item in the checklist links to the relevant settings page where you can complete that step.

Check all five boxes, then click **Yes, I want to publish my deal page**.

Have questions? Click **Reach out to us** at the bottom of that screen to contact Fairmint support.

## What Happens Next

After you click the publish button, an Intercom chat opens with a pre-filled message to the Fairmint team letting them know you're ready to go live. The team will review your setup and complete the publishing process.

Once published, your deal page is live and investors can access it through your custom domain or invitation links. Your fundraise status changes to active, and you can monitor investor activity from your dashboard.
