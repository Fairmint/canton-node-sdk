# Manage Your Investors

The Stakeholders page shows everyone who has signed up through your deal page — investors, team members, and anyone with access to your fundraise.

Go to your [Stakeholders](https://one.fairmint.com/p/{portalId}/stakeholders) page or click **Stakeholders** in the left sidebar.

## Stakeholder List

The page shows two tabs:

- **All** — every person on your cap table, with a count
- **Investors** — filtered to show only investors

The table includes:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| NAME | Person's name |
| COUNTRY | Country of residence |
| PROFILE | Profile completion status |
| SERIES | Which fundraise(s) they're associated with |
| STATUS | Current status (e.g., signed up, invested, blocked) |
| SIGNUP | Date and time they signed up |

Use the **Search by name or email** field to find specific people. Use **Filter by** and **Sort by** dropdowns to organize the list.

## Export Stakeholders

Click **Export Stakeholders** in the top-right corner to download your stakeholder data.

## View a Stakeholder's Profile

Click the arrow icon on any row to open that person's detailed profile, where you can view their investment details, compliance status, and contact information.
