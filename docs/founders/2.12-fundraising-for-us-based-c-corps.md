# Fundraising for US Based C-Corps

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

For US-based C-Corporations looking to raise funds, Fairmint offers robust and flexible options tailored to meet your company's specific needs. Whether you're looking for a straightforward, cost-effective way to fundraise or a more customized and comprehensive approach, we have solutions designed to facilitate your growth.

## Option 1: Free Fundraising

This option is ideal for C-Corps seeking a simple and streamlined fundraising process:

- **Regulation D, Rule 506(b)**: Utilize this rule to fundraise without public solicitation, maintaining privacy and control over who invests in your company.
- **Simple Fundraising Deal Page**: The deal page provided under 506(b) is designed to be straightforward, focusing solely on essential information and quick investment actions, perfect for investors with whom you already have an existing relationship.
- **Use of SAFE Templates**: Access our range of included SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) templates, which are designed to simplify the equity agreement process.
- **Unlimited Fundraising Potential**: There is no cap on the amount you can raise or the number of accredited investors you can include.

## Option 2: Fairmint One

For C-Corps that want the best for their cap table and investors:

- **The Ultimate Fundraising Toolkit**: Fairmint One is your all-in-one solution, providing unparalleled options to tailor your fundraising to exact specifications and needs.
- **Choose Between Reg D 506(b) and 506(c)**: Depending on your need to publicly solicit investors, choose the regulatory framework that best suits your strategy. Use 506(b) for private fundraising within your network or 506(c) for public solicitation and advertising.
- **Customizable Deal Page**: Get a deal page that matches the chosen regulation type, each designed to cater to specific investor interactions and requirements.
- **Flexible Legal Templates**: In addition to using any of our included SAFE templates, you have the option to upload custom legal documents, offering greater flexibility in how you structure your fundraising agreements.
- **Simplified Cap Table**: Aggregate all investor contributions into a single line item on your cap table, streamlining investor management and simplifying equity distribution.
- **Optional Voting Rights Reassignment**: Offer a unique feature where investors can give their voting rights back to you, allowing for greater control over company decisions.
- **Unlimited Fundraising Potential**: There is no cap on the amount you can raise or the number of accredited investors you can include.
