# Manage Your Series

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

After creating a fundraising round or grant series, the **Series Detail** page is your central hub for monitoring activity, managing settings, and taking action. Click any series name in the left menu (under Shares, SAFEs, Warrants, or Grants) to open it.

For an overview of the full fundraising flow, see [Fundraising with Fairmint](2.0-fundraising-with-fairmint.md). To create a new round, see [Create a Fundraise (SAFE)](2.2-create-fundraise-safe.md).

---

## Header & Actions

The top of the page shows:

- **Series name** — with the current status shown next to it (Setup, Ready, Live, Paused, or Closed)
- **Publish** — appears when the series is in Setup or Ready status. Click to go live. See [How to Publish a Fundraise](2.3-publish-fundraise.md) for details.
- **Investor Preview** — opens a preview of your deal page as investors will see it
- **Create new invitation link** — generates a new link for sharing with investors. See [Invite an Investor](6.2-deal-invitation-links.md) for details.
- **External link icon** — opens your public deal page (visible when custom domain is verified)

### "..." Menu

Click the three-dot menu for additional actions:

| Action | When Available | What It Does |
|--------|---------------|--------------|
| **Edit Public Deal Page** | Series not closed | Opens the deal page editor. See [Deal Page Design](2.10-deal-page-design.md). |
| **Pause/Resume** | Live or Paused | Pauses your fundraise (hides it from investors) or resumes it. See [Pause/Resume a Fundraise](2.5-how-to-pause-resume-a-fundraise.md). |
| **Close** | Live or Paused | Permanently ends the fundraising round. See [End a Fundraise](2.6-how-to-end-a-fundraise.md). |
| **Delete** | Setup only | Permanently deletes the series. Cannot be undone. |

---

## Key Metrics

Below the header, metric cards summarize your series at a glance. Which cards appear depends on your series type and terms:

| Metric | When Shown | Description |
|--------|-----------|-------------|
| **Total Raised** | All fundraise series | Total funds raised in this series |
| **Stakeholders** | Always | Number of stakeholders in this series |
| **Share Price** | When price per share > 0 | Price per share for equity series |
| **Valuation Cap** | When set in terms | The valuation cap for SAFE series |
| **Discount** | When set in terms | The discount percentage for SAFE series |
| **MFN** | When instrument is MFN | Indicates Most Favored Nation terms |
| **Token Price** | When token issuance is enabled | Current token price based on latest transaction |

For grant series, only the **Stakeholders** metric is shown.

## Tabs

### Stakeholders

Lists all stakeholders in this series. You can view details for each by clicking their row.

### Investments

Lists all investments in this series with status, amount, and method. You can filter and sort the table, and click any investment to view its full detail.

### Invitation Links

Manage the links you share with investors. Each link can have a passcode, expiration, and custom name. You can create, edit, or deactivate links from here. See [Invite an Investor](6.2-deal-invitation-links.md) for details.

### Activity

Shows a chronological log of all activity on this series — signups, investments, status changes, and more.

### Compliance

Shows your **Form D filing** status for this series:

- **Not Filed** — "Start Form D filing" button links to the filing agent
- **Pending** — your filing is pending SEC approval
- **Filed** — your filing was approved, with a link to view it on EDGAR

This tab is hidden for Soft Commitment series.

### Terms

Displays the deal terms you set when creating the series:

- **Roll up** — whether investors are consolidated into one cap table line
- **Type** — the series type (e.g., Fundraising, Token, Verification)
- **General solicitation** — Yes (506c) or No (506b)
- **Fundraising objective** — target amount. Click the edit icon to change this after creation.
- **Total amount raised** — current total
- **Instrument** — the legal instrument (e.g., SAFE: Valuation Cap, no Discount)
- **Valuation Cap / Discount** — if applicable
- **Token warrant** — if applicable, with purchase price
- **Pre-signed by company** — whether the legal agreement is pre-signed
- **Price per share** — for equity (share) series
- **Pitch deck** — link to view or update your presentation

**Editing terms**: When the series is still in Setup status (not yet published), you can click **"Edit terms"** to modify the deal terms. You can also click **"Edit Legal Agreement"** to update signature fields on your legal contract. *The former standalone article **How to Edit Fundraising Terms** (2.4) is folded into this subsection — the guidance here is the full coverage.*

> **Important**: Once a series is live, most terms are locked and cannot be changed. The **fundraising objective** (target amount) is the only term you can edit on a live series.
