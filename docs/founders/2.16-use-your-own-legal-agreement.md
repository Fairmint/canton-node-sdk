# Use Your Own Legal Agreement

*This article is for both founders and investors using Fairmint.*

Fairmint provides standard investment contracts out of the box, including various YC SAFE templates. However, you may sometimes need a custom legal agreement tailored to your company. This could be a Series A purchase agreement, an equity compensation agreement, or a customized token warrant. Whatever the case, Fairmint enables you to upload and use your own agreements seamlessly within your portal.

## Fairmint Contract Builder

Fairmint includes a built-in legal contract builder, allowing you to create custom legal templates for your investors or employees to sign. It provides a standard set of variables that dynamically pre-fill with the investor's information at the time of signing, ensuring accuracy and efficiency. No need to create a separate contract for each investor. With Fairmint Contract Builder, you can create a single contract template for all your investors.

## Uploading Your Own Legal Agreement in Fairmint

When creating a new Series in Fairmint, select "I want to use my own legal agreement." You will then be prompted to upload your legal agreement, which can consist of one or multiple files. These files can be combined within the contract builder to form a complete agreement.

**Important:** Since you will be inserting variables into your legal agreement, you need to upload a legal agreement with blank spaces for the investor's information and the amount they are investing.

You will need to provide two key details:

1. **What type of financial instrument is your legal agreement about?**
   - **Share** — Direct equity ownership in the company.
   - **Convertible** — A contract that converts into equity under specific conditions.

2. **Is your legal agreement already signed by the company?**
   - **Yes** — The agreement has already been signed by the company, and only the investor's signature is missing.
   - **No** — The CEO (or an authorized representative) will counter-sign after the investor has signed.

## Available Variables

| Variable Name | Description |
|---------------|-------------|
| DollarAmountTotal | The amount that the investor is investing |
| InvestorEmail | The email of the investor |
| InvestorFullAddress | The full address of the investor |
| InvestorName | The name of the individual or the legal entity investing |
| InvestorSigner | The name of the individual signing the legal agreement |
| InvestorTitle | The title of the signer in the legal entity |
| PurchasedSharesAmount | The total amount of shares purchased (not available for convertibles) |
| ShareAmountTotal | Amount of underlying shares granted (equity compensation only) |
| SignatureDate | The date of signature |
| StartDate | Effective start date (equity compensation only) |
| CustomVariable | Reach out to us if your legal agreement requires a custom variable |

**Important:** For those variables to be pre-filled, you need to assign them to the "Sender" role in the editor.
