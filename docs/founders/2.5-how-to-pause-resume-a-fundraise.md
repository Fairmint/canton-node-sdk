# How to Pause/Resume a Fundraise

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

Whether you need to pause to make adjustments or resume to continue attracting investments, the process is straightforward. Here's how to toggle the status of your fundraising directly from your Fairmint admin account.

## Steps to Pause or Resume Your Fundraise

1. **Access Your Offering Page**: Log into your admin account and navigate to the fundraise page you wish to modify.
2. **Navigate to Advanced Management**: Scroll to the very bottom of the page and open the "Advanced Management" dropdown menu.
3. **Toggle Your Fundraising Status**: Click on "Pause Fundraising" to halt your campaign and make it invisible to potential investors, or click "Resume Fundraising" to reactivate it and open up to investments again.
