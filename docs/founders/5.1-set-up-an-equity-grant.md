# Set Up an Equity Grant

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

This article walks you through creating an equity grant series with a vesting schedule in Fairmint. A grant series defines the terms for a group of grants — the compensation type, vesting schedule, and legal agreement. Once created, you can issue individual grants to recipients from that series.

## Create a Grant Series

1. Click **+ New** in the left menu
2. Select **Grant equity**
3. You'll see the **"Set the terms of your grant series"** form. Each field has a detailed explanation on the right side of the screen.

Fill in:

1. **Who do you want to grant equity to?** — give your grant series a descriptive name (e.g., "2025 Employee Incentive Plan", "Advisors", "Contractors")

2. **Compensation Type** — this determines how the grant is treated for tax and legal purposes:
   - RSUs (Restricted Stock Units)
   - Options
   - Options (ISO) — Incentive Stock Options
   - Options (NSO) — Non-qualified Stock Options

3. **Vesting Schedule** — defines when recipients gain the right to their equity:
   - Four Years + 1 Year Cliff Period (most common)
   - Four Years
   - Three Years
   - One Year + 1 Month Cliff Period
   - 2 months w/ 1 month cliff period
   - No Vesting

4. **Is your compensation agreement already signed by the company?**
   - Yes, the company has pre-signed the agreement — use this if your company has already signed the grant agreement template
   - No, the company needs to sign each agreement — use this if the company signer needs to sign each individual grant

5. **Upload PDF documents** — optionally upload your compensation agreement or related documents

Click **Save and continue** when done.

## Set Up Your Legal Agreement Template

After clicking **Save and continue**, a legal agreement editor opens. This is where you configure the signature fields on your compensation agreement:

- Drag **Signature** and **Initials** fields onto the document where recipients need to sign
- Auto-fill fields (**Date signed**, **Full name**, **Email address**, **Company**, **Title**) are populated automatically for each recipient
- You can also add standard fields like **Textbox**, **Checkbox**, **Dropdown**, or **Radio group** for custom inputs

Click **Save template** when done. You can **Preview** to see how it looks before saving.

## Publishing Your Grant Series

After your template is saved, the grant series appears under **Grants** in the left menu in draft mode. To make it available for issuing grants:

1. Open the grant series detail page
2. Click **Publish** in the top-right corner
3. A chat window opens with the Fairmint team — send the pre-filled message to request review
4. The Fairmint team reviews your setup and notifies you when it's approved

> **Note:** Unlike fundraising rounds, grant series don't go live automatically. The Fairmint team reviews each one before activation.

## Creating Invitation Links

From the grant series detail page, click **+ New equity grant** to create an invitation link for a recipient. You'll provide their name, email, and grant quantity. The link is sent to the recipient to begin the signing process.

When creating a link, you can optionally check **"Require tax form (W-9 / W-8BEN)"**. When enabled:

- **US-based recipients** will sign a W-9 alongside the compensation agreement
- **Non-US recipients** will sign a W-8BEN instead
- Both forms are completed in a **single signing session** — the recipient doesn't need to sign separately

The tax form status appears on the invitation link table and the individual grant detail page.

## After Creating a Grant Series

Once your grant series is live, you can issue individual equity grants to specific recipients from the grant series detail page.

For details on how vesting works, cliff periods, and available schedules, see [Understanding Vesting Schedules](5.3-understanding-vesting-schedules.md).
