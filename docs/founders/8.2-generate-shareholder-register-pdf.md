# Generate a Shareholder Register PDF

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

This article explains how to generate a PDF shareholder register from your Documents page.

## Before You Start

The Generate Report button appears once your cap table has been set up.

## How to Generate a Report

1. Go to your **Documents** page

2. Click **Generate Report** at the top of the page

3. Configure your report settings:
   - **Report Type** — select "Shareholder Register" (this is the only option currently available). The description reads: *"Generate a PDF shareholder register of all shareholders, including legal names, entity types, share classes, shares held, and ownership percentages."*
   - **Register as of** — choose the date for your report. Defaults to today. The helper text reads: *"The register will reflect the cap table as of this date."* You can select any past date to see your cap table as it existed on that date.
   - **Optional Sections** — check the boxes to include additional detail:
     - **Include share class details** — adds voting rights, liquidation preferences, and other stock class information
     - **Include transaction history** — shows all stock transactions (issuances, transfers, cancellations) up to the selected date

4. Click **Generate Report**

The PDF downloads automatically to your browser. It is also saved to the **Shareholder Registers** folder on your Documents page for future access.

## What's in the PDF

Your shareholder register includes the following sections:

### Header

Your company logo (if uploaded) and the title "Shareholder Register" with your company name and the as-of date. Pages 2 and beyond show a smaller header with your company name and date.

### Company Information

Two columns showing:

- Legal Name, State of Incorporation, Country of Formation, EIN
- Authorized Shares (broken down by class), Par Value, Date of Incorporation

### Share Class Details (if selected)

When you check **Include share class details**, each share class gets its own sub-section listing:

- Voting rights (e.g., "1 vote per share" or "Non-voting")
- Par value
- Authorized shares
- Liquidation preference
- Conversion rights
- Price per share, participation cap, and seniority (when applicable)

### Shareholder Register Table

The main table with one row per stakeholder per share class, sorted by ownership percentage (largest first):

| Column | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Shareholder Name | Jane Smith |
| Address | 123 Main St, New York, NY 10001, USA |
| Class | Common Stock |
| Shares Issued | 100,000 |
| % Ownership | 25.00% |
| Price per Share | $0.001 |
| Issue Date | January 15, 2024 |
| Cert ID | abc-123-def |

A **Totals** row at the bottom shows the combined shares issued and total ownership percentage.

### Transaction History (if selected)

When you check **Include transaction history**, a table shows all stock transactions up to the as-of date:

| Column | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Date | March 1, 2024 |
| Transaction Type | Initial Issuance, Transfer, Cancellation, Repurchase, Conversion, etc. |
| Shareholder | Jane Smith |
| Shares | 100,000 |
| Certificate ID | abc-123-def |

### Summary Statistics

Two columns showing:

- Total Authorized Shares, Total Issued & Outstanding, Reserved for Future Issuance
- Number of Shareholders, Number of Record Holders, Fully Diluted Shares

### Certification

A formal statement certifying that the register is a true and complete copy of the stock ledger, followed by a signature line with the designated signer's name, title, and the as-of date.

### Footer

Every page shows *"Generated by Fairmint — https://fairmint.com"* on the left and the page number on the right. The report ends with a disclaimer that it is for informational purposes and should be verified with legal counsel.

## Accessing Previous Reports

All generated shareholder registers are saved to the **Shareholder Registers** folder on your Documents page. Click the folder to view and download previously generated reports.
