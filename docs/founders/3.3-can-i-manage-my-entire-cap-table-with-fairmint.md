# Can I Manage My Entire Cap Table with Fairmint?

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

Yes — Fairmint provides cap table management. You can import your existing equity history, track transactions, and view your ownership structure.

To get started, see [Import Your Cap Table](3.1-upload-import-cap-table.md). Once imported, you can explore your cap table through:

- [Summary View](3.4-cap-table-summary-view.md) — ownership by share class
- [Holder View](3.5-cap-table-holder-view.md) — ownership by stakeholder
- [Timeline View](3.6-cap-table-timeline-view.md) — chronological equity events

You can also model scenarios with [Pro-Forma Cap Table](3.7-pro-forma-cap-table.md).
