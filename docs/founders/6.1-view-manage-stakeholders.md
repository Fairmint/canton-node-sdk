# View and Manage Stakeholders

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

The Stakeholders page shows everyone associated with your portal — investors, team members, and anyone who has signed up through your deal page or invitation links.

Click **Stakeholders** in the left menu to open this page.

## Stakeholder List

The page has two tabs:

- **All** — every stakeholder in your portal (with count)
- **Investors** — filtered to show only investors (with count)

The table columns:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **NAME** | Person's name with avatar |
| **COUNTRY** | Country of residence with flag |
| **PROFILE** | Eligibility and accreditation labels (e.g., Prohibited, International, Not accredited, Self-accredited) |
| **SERIES** | Which fundraise(s) they're associated with (color-coded labels; hover for details) |
| **STATUS** | Row of icons — hover over each for details on verification progress: legal name, tax residency, phone, KYC/AML, wallet, KYB (for companies) |
| **SIGNUP** | Date and time they signed up |

### Search, Filter & Sort

- **Search by name or email** — text search field
- **Filter by** — dropdown with options: All, Action required, Identity verified, Legal name not added, Tax residency info not added, Rejected, Blocked (only shown on the **All** tab)
- **Sort by** — Signup date descending or Signup date ascending

Stakeholders requiring action are highlighted with a yellow left border.

### Pagination

The table is paginated with rows-per-page options: 25, 50, 100, 250, 500, or 1,000.

## Export Stakeholders

Click **Export Stakeholders** in the top-right corner to download your stakeholder data as a CSV file. On smaller screens, the button label is shortened to **Export**.

## View a Stakeholder's Profile

Click anywhere on a stakeholder row to open their detailed profile page. The profile page has the following tabs:

- **Profile** — contact information, personal details, and account settings
- **Legal entity** — company information (only shown if the stakeholder is an organization)
- **Identity (KYC)** — identity verification status and documents (hidden for company stakeholders)
- **Anti Money Laundering (AML)** — AML screening results (hidden for company stakeholders)
- **Investments** — all investments made by this stakeholder
- **Grants** — all equity grants received by this stakeholder
- **Activity** — timeline of actions and events for this stakeholder
