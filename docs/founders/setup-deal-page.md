# Set Up Your Deal Page

Your deal page is what investors see when they visit your fundraise. After creating a fundraise, you land directly in the deal page editor where you can customize the content.

You can return to the editor anytime by clicking on a fundraise from your [Series](https://one.fairmint.com/p/{portalId}/series) page.

## Page Layout

The deal page editor has three columns:

- **Left**: Your series card and branding
- **Center**: Editable title, CEO quote, and tabbed content sections
- **Right sidebar**: Investment widget, deal terms summary, Form D status, and "They backed us" section

The top bar has **View as an investor** (preview), **Publish**, and **Save & Close**.

## Headline

Click the title area at the top to open the **Headline** dialog. Type your catchy title for the deal page — this is the first thing investors read. Click **Save** when done.

## Series Card and Logo

Click the series card (the dark card with your logo) to open the branding dialog:

- **Select your background** — choose between a brand-color-based design or a holographic style
- **Upload your logo** — a transparent squared PNG image or SVG is recommended

## CEO Quote

Click the quote area to open the quote editor. Write a short personal statement highlighting your vision and the value of the investment opportunity. The signer name and title shown are pulled from your company settings.

## Social Media

Click the globe icon to manage social media links. These are shared across all your fundraising pages. Click **Go to Settings** to edit them.

## Content Tabs

The deal page has four content tabs. Click on a section to open its editor.

### Team

Add the people behind your company. There are two sections:

- **Founders** — each entry has Name, Role, and Story fields. Use **+ Add co-founder** to add more.
- **Team members** — each entry has Name and Role. Use **+ Add team member** to add more.

### Learn More

Add external links to help investors research your company — articles, presentations, press coverage, videos. Each link has a display text and URL. Use **+ Add link** to add more. Your pitch deck link appears here by default.

### Testimonials

Showcase endorsements from clients, partners, or existing investors. Each testimonial has Name, Role, and Story fields. Use **+ Add testimonial** to add more.

### Risks

Displays standard investment risk disclosures under a **Disclosures** section. This is pre-filled boilerplate language required for fundraising compliance.

## They Backed Us

Click the "They backed us" section in the right sidebar to feature notable investors or supporters. Each entry has an Investor name and Role field. Use **+ Add investor** to add more. This helps build credibility with potential investors.

## Save and Preview

- Click **Save & Close** to save your changes and return to the series detail page
- Click **View as an investor** to preview how your deal page looks to investors
- When you're happy with the page, click **Publish** to make it live (see **Publish a Fundraise**)
