# Filings (SEC Form D)

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

The Filings page helps you track and complete your SEC Form D filing — a required filing for most Regulation D offerings. Go to **Tax & Compliance > Filings** in the left menu.

## What Is a Form D?

When you raise money under Regulation D (Rule 506(b) or 506(c)), you're required to file a Form D with the SEC within 15 days of making your first sale. Fairmint simplifies this process through a partner filing agent.

## What You'll See

The page shows a centered card with the **SEC seal** image and the heading **"Form D"** alongside a colored status label. Your filing will be in one of three states:

| Status | Label | What To Do |
|--------|-------|------------|
| **Not filed** | Orange "Not filed" + **"I am interested to file"** button | Click the button to proceed to payment |
| **Pending** | Blue "Pending" | Wait for SEC processing — this can take a few business days |
| **Complete** | Green "Complete" + **"View Filing"** link | Click to view your filing on the SEC's EDGAR system |

## Pricing

Clicking **"I am interested to file"** takes you to a Stripe checkout page:

- **SEC EDGAR Filing (Form D)** — **$999.00** (one-time)
- Payment methods: Credit/debit card or Klarna
- After payment, Fairmint's SEC filing agent handles the EDGAR submission on your behalf

The filing status is tied to your first fundraising series. If you haven't created a fundraising series yet, there won't be a filing to track.
