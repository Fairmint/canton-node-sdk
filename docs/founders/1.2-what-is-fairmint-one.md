# What is Fairmint One?

*This article is for both founders and investors using Fairmint.*

Fairmint One is a comprehensive equity management solution designed to empower companies with the ultimate toolkit for managing their equity-related activities. With a one-time payment of $5,000, Fairmint One provides an array of features tailored to streamline and enhance your fundraising and equity management processes.

## Key Features of Fairmint One

### 1. Comprehensive Fundraising Software

- **Investor Management**: Manage all your investor interactions and data from a single, easy-to-use dashboard.
- **Document Signing**: Facilitate easy and secure digital signing of documents directly within the platform.
- **Legal Templates**: Access a variety of legal templates that simplify the process of preparing necessary documentation for both 506(b) and 506(c) offerings.
- **Custom Legal Documents**: Have the flexibility to use your own custom legal documents tailored to your specific needs.
- **Beautiful Deal Pages**: Create and share engaging deal pages that clearly present your investment opportunities to potential investors.
- **Consolidation of Investors**: Simplify your cap table by consolidating multiple investors into a single cap table line item, enhancing clarity and manageability.
- Much more!

### 2. Cap Table Management

Gain full control over your cap table with tools that allow you to manage share allocations, transfers, and adjustments seamlessly.

### 3. Equity Grants and Employee Stock Ownership Plan (ESOP)

Manage and distribute equity grants and set up an ESOP for your employees, fostering a culture of ownership and aligning interests within your company.

## Lifetime Access

**No Recurring Fees**: Pay once and enjoy lifetime access to all these features for the life of your company. Fairmint One is designed to grow with your business, providing continual value without additional costs.

## Benefits of Using Fairmint One

- **All-in-One Platform**: By consolidating all your equity management needs into one platform, you reduce complexity and increase efficiency.
- **Cost-Effective**: With a one-time payment, you eliminate the ongoing costs typically associated with equity management software.
- **Customization and Flexibility**: Tailor the platform to meet your specific legal and operational needs, giving you greater control over your fundraising and equity management strategies.
