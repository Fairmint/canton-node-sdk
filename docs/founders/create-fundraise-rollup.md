# Create a Fundraise (Roll Up)

A roll up fundraise consolidates your investors into a single line on your cap table. Instead of each investor appearing separately, they're grouped under one entry managed by Fairmint's SEC-registered transfer agent.

This is useful when raising from a large number of angels, friends and family, or community investors — it keeps your cap table clean for future rounds.

## Requirements

- **Fairmint One** membership required (contact the team to upgrade)

## How to Create

Follow the same steps as creating a regular SAFE fundraise (see **Create a Fundraise (SAFE)**), with one change:

1. On the **"Set the terms of your fundraising round"** form, set the first dropdown to: **"Yes, I want all investors of this Series into one line on my cap table"**
2. Fill in the remaining fields (series name, amount, instrument, etc.) as usual
3. Click **Save and continue**

Everything else — instrument selection, terms, pitch deck, publishing — works the same as a standard fundraise.

## What Happens After

When investors participate in a roll-up fundraise, they go through the same investment flow but their holdings are consolidated into one cap table entry rather than appearing as individual lines.
