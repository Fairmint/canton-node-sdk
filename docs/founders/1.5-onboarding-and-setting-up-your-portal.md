# Onboarding & Setting Up Your Portal

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

When you first create a Fairmint account, you'll be guided through a quick setup wizard to get your portal ready. This article walks you through each step.

> **Need help?** The Intercom chat widget (bottom-right corner) is available on every screen, including during onboarding. Feel free to reach out to our team at any point.

---

## Before You Start

You'll need:
- Your company's legal name and website
- Your name and role (e.g., CEO, CMO, Director)
- Your phone number
- Your company's incorporation date and entity type

The entire process takes about 5 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Hello There!

The first screen asks: **"Welcome! What should we call you?"**

Enter your:
- **First name**
- **Last name**

A progress bar at the top tracks your progress through the wizard. Click **Continue** to proceed.

---

## Step 2: Company Info

The heading reads: **"Company info"** with the subtitle: *"Tell us about your company"*

Fill in the following fields:
- **Company name** — your legal entity name
- **Company website** — pre-filled with `https://`, just add your domain
- **Phone number** — select your country code (defaults to US +1) and enter the number
- **Your role** — dropdown to select your title (e.g., CEO, CMO, CTO, Director)
- **Is your company a US C-Corp?** — dropdown to select your entity type. If you select "Foreign company," an additional country field appears.
- **Company incorporation date** — select the date your company was incorporated

Click **Continue** to proceed.

---

## Step 3: Terms & Agreements

The heading reads: **"Please review and confirm your agreement to the following items."**

Three checkboxes:
- **"I have read and accept the Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, & the Site Content Terms of Use and agree to using relevant third party under these terms."** — required (includes links to each document)
- **"I have authority to act on behalf of the organization, and to bind the organization to these agreements."** — required
- **"I consent to receive communications from Fairmint."** — optional

The **Continue** button stays greyed out until the two required checkboxes are checked.

---

## Step 4: Tell Us More

The heading reads: **"Tell us more"** with the subtitle: *"Let's tailor your experience to your needs."*

Answer four questions using dropdowns:
- **What is your industry?**
- **What is your company size?**
- **How much did you already raise?**
- **What is your company's stage?**

This information helps Fairmint customize your portal experience. Click **Continue** to proceed.

---

## Step 5: Help Us Tailor Your Experience

The heading reads: **"Help us tailor your experience"** with the subtitle: *"Those details will ensure you get the best experience from Fairmint."*

- **How many people are on your cap table?** — dropdown
- **Where is your cap table managed today?** — dropdown (spreadsheet, another platform, etc.)
- **What is your fundraising timeline?** — slider ranging from "Not currently raising" to actively fundraising
- **Are you planning to grant equity soon?** — slider ranging from "Not now" to actively granting
- **What's your top priority with Fairmint?** — select one: **Raise Capital**, **Manage Cap Table**, **Grant Equity**, or **Just exploring**

Click **Continue** to complete onboarding.

---

## Welcome to Fairmint!

After completing the wizard, you'll see a welcome screen with the Fairmint logo and a rotating card showing sample equity identities from well-known companies (Stripe, OpenAI, SpaceX, etc.) with the tagline: **"with stylish equity identity"**.

The message reads: *"Fairmint offers the simplest way to raise from one or more investors, with one line on your cap table!"*

Click **Continue** to enter your portal.

You'll be taken to **Settings** where you can complete your company profile. The Settings page shows cards for:
- **Company information** — pre-filled from onboarding (name, website, country, entity type, incorporation date)
- **Signer information** — your name, title, and email
- **Emails** — compliance and contact email addresses (shows a warning icon until completed)
- **Social media accounts** — links to your company's social profiles (warning icon until completed)
- **Legal information** — (warning icon until completed)
- **Branding** — customize your portal's appearance (warning icon until completed)

A banner at the top reminds you: *"Don't forget to complete the missing information below before publishing your deal page."*

---

## For Invited Team Members

If you were invited to an existing portal by a team member, you'll see a different, simpler screen.

The heading reads: **"How should we call you?"** with the subtitle: *"As a new team member joining the team on Fairmint, we just need a few details to get things rolling."*

Enter your:
- **First name**
- **Last name**

A rotating card shows sample equity identities alongside the form. Click **Enter Fairmint** to go straight to the portal.
