# State Fees (Blue Sky)

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

The State Fees page helps you manage state-level regulatory fees for your offering, commonly known as "blue sky" fees. Go to **Tax & Compliance > State Fees** in the left menu.

## What Are State Fees?

In addition to the federal Form D filing, many U.S. states require you to file a notice and pay a fee when selling securities to residents of that state. These are called "blue sky" fees because they come from state-level securities regulations (historically known as "blue sky laws").

## What You'll See

The page shows a centered card with the **NASAA** (North American Securities Administrators Association) logo, followed by "Popular States: **AZ, CO, CT, CA, NY, DC**" and a colored status label.

| Status | Label | What To Do |
|--------|-------|------------|
| **Not filed** | Orange "Not filed" + **"I plan to raise from all these States"** button | Click the button to proceed to payment |
| **Prepaid** | Green "Prepaid" | Nothing to do — your state fees are complete |

## Pricing

Clicking **"I plan to raise from all these States"** takes you to a Stripe checkout page:

- **State Regulatory Fees (Blue Sky Filing Fees)** — **$1,999.00** (one-time)
- Description: "State regulatory fees, also known as Blue Sky filing fees, vary by state or province. If one or more investors from a state invest in your company, filing is mandatory."
- Payment methods: Credit/debit card or Klarna

The fee status is tied to your first fundraising series. If you haven't created a fundraising series yet, there won't be any fees to track.
