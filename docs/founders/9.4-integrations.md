# Integrations

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

Enhancing your Fairmint platform with integrations can streamline your operations and improve communication with your stakeholders.

## Available Integrations

- **Slack:** Receive real-time notifications of activities from your investment portal directly in your Slack workspace. This keeps your team updated on all essential actions and interactions within your platform.

- **Intercom:** Engage directly with potential investors on your deal page using the Intercom chatbot. This tool enhances investor support and engagement, providing a direct line of communication.

- **Telegram:** Receive notifications about your investment portal activity directly in a Telegram chat or group. This integration is only visible when the Telegram bot is configured for your environment.

## Steps to Set Up Integrations

1. Click your **profile picture** in the top-right corner and select **Settings**
2. Click the **Integrations** tab

### Slack

Enter your Slack webhook URL in the provided field. This connects your Fairmint portal to your Slack workspace for real-time notifications.

### Intercom

Enter your Intercom app ID in the designated field. This links your Intercom account to your deal page, enabling the chat widget for investor communication.

### Telegram

To set up Telegram notifications:

1. **For a personal chat**: Message the Fairmint bot on Telegram to get your Chat ID
2. **For a group**: Add the Fairmint notifications bot to your Telegram group, give it administrator rights, then type `/start` in the group to receive your Chat ID
3. Enter the Chat ID in the **Telegram Chat ID** field and click **Save**
