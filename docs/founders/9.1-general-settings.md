# General Settings

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

The General tab in Settings is where you manage your company's core information, branding, and contact details. Click your **profile picture** in the top-right corner, select **Settings** — the General tab opens by default.

If any section has missing information, you'll see a warning icon next to its title and a banner at the top:

> "Don't forget to complete the missing information below before publishing your deal page."

Each section has a pencil icon you can click to edit it.

## Company Information

Displays your company's key details:

- **Legal name** and **display name** (shown as "Legal Name (Display Name)")
- **Website** — click to visit
- **Address** — street, zip code, city, state (US only), country
- **EIN** (US companies) or **Tax ID** (non-US companies)
- **Legal entity type**
- **Incorporation state** (US only) — hover the info icon for an explanation
- **Governing law** (US only) — hover the info icon for an explanation
- **Incorporation date**

Click the pencil icon to update any of these.

## Signer Information

The person authorized to sign legal documents on behalf of your company:

- **First name**
- **Last name**
- **Title** (e.g., CEO, CFO)
- **Email**

This information appears on generated agreements and shareholder registers.

## Emails

Contact email addresses shown to your investors:

- **Compliance** — for compliance-related inquiries
- **Contact** — general contact email

Shows "N / A" if not set.

## Social Media Accounts

Links to your company's social media profiles. Each platform shows its logo:

- **Facebook**
- **Instagram**
- **LinkedIn**
- **Twitter**
- **Farcaster**

If a link is set, you can click it to visit the profile. If not, it shows "N / A". If none are set, you'll see: *"Keep your community up to date by adding links to your social media accounts."*

## Legal Information

Links to your company's legal documents:

- **Terms of Service** — click to view
- **Privacy Policy** — click to view

If not set, you'll see: *"Click the edit button to add links to your terms of service and privacy policy."*

## Branding

Your company logos used across Fairmint:

- **Square logo, colored** — used for emails, the browser tab icon on your deal page, and more
- **Rectangle logo, colored** — used for the signup and login pages of your deal page

Each logo shows a small preview. If a logo hasn't been uploaded, it shows "Not uploaded yet."

If no logos are set at all, you'll see: *"Click the edit button to add various versions of your company logo, ensuring your deal page reflect your brand's unique style."*

## Settings Tabs Overview

The Settings page has five tabs. A dot appears next to any tab that has incomplete setup tasks.

| Tab | What It Contains |
|-----|-----------------|
| **General** | Company info, signer, emails, social media, legal info, branding (this article) |
| **Team** | Invite and manage team members (see [Team Management](9.3-team-management.md)) |
| **Bank details** | Bank account information for receiving investment funds (not shown for fund accounts) |
| **Integrations** | Connect Slack, Telegram, and Intercom |
| **Custom domain** | Set up a custom domain for your deal page |
