# Create a Fundraise (SAFE)

This article walks you through creating a SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) fundraising round in Fairmint.

## Start a New Fundraise

1. Click **+ New** in the left sidebar
2. Select **Raise capital**
3. If you see a Fairmint One upgrade prompt, click **Continue** to proceed

## Set the Terms

You'll see the **"Set the terms of your fundraising round"** form. Each field has a detailed explanation on the right side of the screen — click into any field to see it.

Fill in:

1. **Roll up investors** — whether to consolidate investors into one cap table line (Fairmint One only)
2. **Series name** — name your round (e.g., "Series Pre-Seed")
3. **Amount to raise** — your target in dollars
4. **Solicit and advertise** — whether to publicly market your fundraise (506c) or keep it private (506b)
5. **Instrument** — choose your SAFE type. This can't be changed later:
   - Safe: Discount, no Valuation Cap
   - Safe: Discount, no Valuation Cap + Token Warrant
   - Safe: MFN, no Valuation Cap, no Discount
   - Safe: Valuation Cap, no Discount
   - Safe: Valuation Cap, no Discount + Token Warrant
   - I have my own legal agreement

   Each SAFE comes with pre-built Reg D and Reg S legal agreements you can preview.

6. **Additional terms** — depending on your instrument choice, you may see fields for discount rate, valuation cap, token warrant, pro rata rights, investor proxy agreement, and pitch deck upload

Click **Save and continue** when done.

## After Creation

Your fundraise starts in setup mode and isn't visible to investors yet. Before publishing, you can customize your deal page, set investment limits, and create invitation links.
