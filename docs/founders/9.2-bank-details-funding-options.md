# How to Receive Funds (Bank and Crypto)

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

Fairmint does not custody any investments. Funds are transferred directly from investors to your designated bank account or cryptocurrency wallet. Before investors can send funds, you need to configure your payment methods.

Click your **profile picture** in the top-right corner, select **Settings**, then click the **Bank details** tab to set up your receiving options. These details are presented to investors after they sign the legal agreement.

You can add bank details using the form, upload a PDF with your banking instructions, or both.

## Bank Transfer

### Domestic

Fill in your U.S. bank account details:

- **Type of account** — checking or savings (dropdown)
- **ABA Routing number**
- **Account number**
- **Bank name** and **Bank address**
- **Address as registered with your bank**

Click **Save bank information** when done.

### Upload Bank Instructions (Alternative)

Instead of filling out the form, you can upload a PDF with all your banking transfer instructions for investors to download. Click **Upload bank info (PDF)**, select your file, then click **Save bank info**.

### International

Choose whether international investors should use your domestic account or a different one:

- **Use the Domestic account** — same details as above
- **Use a different account for international transfers** — enter separate international banking details

### SEPA Transfer

For European investors paying via SEPA, click **Add information** to enter your SEPA bank details.

## Digital Currency

You can accept crypto payments by entering your wallet addresses. Each wallet type is optional — leave the field empty if you don't want to accept that payment method.

### Ethereum Wallet

Accepts **USDC on Ethereum mainnet**. Enter your Ethereum wallet address and click **Save Ethereum recipient wallet**.

### Solana Wallet

Accepts **USDC, USDT, or PYUSD on Solana**. Enter your Solana wallet address and click **Save Solana recipient wallet**.

### Canton Wallet

Accepts **Canton Coin**. Enter your Canton wallet address and click **Save Canton recipient wallet**.

## What Investors See

After an investor signs the legal agreement for your fundraise, they're shown the payment options you've configured. They choose how to send funds (bank transfer or crypto) and follow the instructions you've provided. You can track incoming investments from the **Investments** tab on your series detail page.
