# Import Your Cap Table

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

Fairmint can build your cap table automatically from your existing documents. Upload your files and AI extracts every transaction, stakeholder, and security.

**Note:** Your cap table is updated in real-time as new investments are processed through Fairmint.

## How to Upload

1. Go to **Cap Table > [Timeline View](3.6-cap-table-timeline-view.md)** in the left menu
2. Click **+ Add** in the top-right corner
3. Click **Choose files to upload** and select your documents

You can upload multiple files at once.

## What to Upload

The system accepts any documents related to your company's equity history:

- **Stock purchase agreements** — share issuances and transfers
- **SAFEs & convertible notes** — outstanding convertible instruments
- **Board consents & resolutions** — authorizations for equity issuances
- **Option grant letters** — employee stock option grants
- **Cap table spreadsheets (XLSX)** — exports from tools like Carta, Pulley, or AngelList, or your own spreadsheets
- **409A valuations** — fair market value appraisals

The more complete your documentation, the more accurate your cap table will be.

## What Happens After Upload

After you upload documents, Fairmint's AI processes them and extracts the relevant cap table data — stakeholders, share classes, transaction dates, and amounts. New entries appear on your [Timeline](3.6-cap-table-timeline-view.md).

You can review the extracted data, edit individual events, and add more documents at any time by clicking **+ Add** again.

Once you're satisfied with the extracted data, click **Apply** in the Timeline toolbar to commit the events to your live cap table.

## Cap Table Views

Your cap table has three views in the left menu:

- **[Summary View](3.4-cap-table-summary-view.md)** — ownership structure by share class, with Export, As-Of, and Pro-Forma tools
- **[Holder View](3.5-cap-table-holder-view.md)** — ownership grouped by stakeholder
- **[Timeline View](3.6-cap-table-timeline-view.md)** — chronological history of all equity events, with document upload and event editing
