# Later Stages — Can I Use Fairmint?

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

Yes — Fairmint supports companies at any stage, including mature organizations considering an IPO.

## What Later-Stage Companies Use Fairmint For

- **Priced equity rounds** — create a Shares series for Series A, B, or later rounds with a set share price
- **Cap table consolidation** — import your existing cap table from spreadsheets and equity documents and manage everything in one place
- **Roll Up investments** — consolidate a large number of investors into a single cap table line, keeping your table clean
- **Custom legal agreements** — upload your own legal documents if the built-in SAFE templates don't fit your round structure
- **Equity grants** — manage employee stock options and RSUs with vesting schedules

## Transitioning to Fairmint

If you have previously raised funding or issued equity, you can still use Fairmint. You can import your existing cap table and equity history into the platform. Work with a qualified attorney to ensure a proper transition.
