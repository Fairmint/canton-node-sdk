# Dashboard Overview

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

Your dashboard is the first thing you see when you log into Fairmint Studio. It gives you a snapshot of your company's equity, fundraising, and stakeholder activity — all in one place.

---

## Ownership Chart

The top-left section shows a **donut chart** of your equity distribution, broken down by share class (e.g., Common Stock, Preferred Stock). Below the chart, a legend lists each class with its color and percentage.

The heading shows your **total outstanding shares**.

**If your cap table isn't set up yet**, you'll see a placeholder chart with the message *"Set up your cap table — Get complete visibility into your company's equity distribution."* and a **Get Started** button. See [Import Your Cap Table](3.1-upload-import-cap-table.md) for next steps.

---

## Tasks

The top-right panel shows your **Tasks** — action items that need attention before your portal is fully set up. Each task has a description and an **X** button to dismiss it.

Common tasks include:
- **Bank information needed** — add bank details for receiving funds
- **Add the emails to use** — set compliance and contact emails
- **Social media accounts** — add your company's social links
- **Legal information** — complete your legal details
- **Branding** — customize your portal's appearance
- **Company info** — finish filling in company details

Operational tasks may also appear as activity happens:
- Issue tokens, review KYC/KYB/AML, check bank transfers

Tasks update in real time. When there are no tasks, you'll see: *"There are no tasks to do right now. You'll see a badge on this icon when there are."*

Clicking a task takes you to the relevant Settings page. See [General Settings](9.1-general-settings.md) for details.

---

## Key Metrics

Six metric cards appear below the ownership chart:

| Metric | What It Shows |
|--------|---------------|
| **Amount Raised** | Total capital raised across all series. Hover for breakdown by stocks and convertibles. Warrant purchase prices are not included — only actual investment capital counts toward this total. |
| **Stakeholders** | Number of stakeholders on your cap table |
| **ESOP Shares** | Total shares reserved under your stock plan |
| **Valuation** | Your company's latest known valuation (hidden if below $100,000) |
| **Share Price** | Current price per share. Hover for per-class breakdown if multiple classes exist. |
| **Authorized Shares** | Total shares your company is authorized to issue |

All metrics show a dash (**–**) until data is available. Hover over the info icon on any metric for more details.

---

## Fundraising

The middle-left section shows your **live fundraising progress**.

**When you have an active series**: a donut chart shows **"Raised so far"** and **"Available"** amounts for each series. If you have multiple series, tabs let you switch between them. A link at the bottom (e.g., **"See TW shares series details →"**) takes you to the series details page.

**When no series exists**: you'll see a *"Set up a fundraising round"* button. Choosing it opens the series creation form. See [Create a New Series](2.1-create-fundraise-rollup.md) for details.

---

## Equity Grants

The middle-right section shows your **equity grant summary**.

**When grants exist**: a horizontal stacked bar shows **Vested**, **Issued**, and **Unallocated** shares — each with a share count and info icon. A **"See grant details →"** link at the bottom takes you to the full equity grants page.

**When no grants exist**: you'll see a *"Create equity grant"* button. Choosing it opens the grant creation form. See [Set Up an Equity Grant](5.1-set-up-an-equity-grant.md) for details.

---

## Latest Activity

Three cards at the bottom show your most recent activity:

- **Latest Signups** — the 5 most recent stakeholders. Click any row to view their details. **"See all latest signups >"** links to the full Stakeholders page.
- **Latest Investments** — the 5 most recent investments, showing investor name, amount, series name, and a status icon. **"See all latest investments >"** links to the Investments page.
- **Latest Equity Grants** — the 5 most recent grants, showing grantee name, share count, and vesting status icon. **"See all latest equity grants >"** links to the Equity Grants page.

When empty, each card shows a message like *"No stakeholders found."*, *"No investments found."*, or *"No equity grants found."*

All three sections update in real time as new stakeholders sign up, investments come in, or grants are created.
