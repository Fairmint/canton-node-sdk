# Team Management

*This article is for founders and admins using Fairmint Studio.*

The Team tab in Settings lets you invite team members and manage their access. Click your **profile picture** in the top-right corner, select **Settings**, then click the **Team** tab.

## Invite Members

At the top of the page, use the invite form to add new team members:

1. Enter their **email address**
2. Select a **Job Title** from the dropdown:
   - CEO, COO, CMO, CFO, CTO, CRO
   - Director, Founder, co-founder, co-CEO
   - CLO, General Counsel, Support, Lawyer, Compliance, Other
3. Click **Send Invite**

A confirmation message appears when the invitation is sent.

## Team Members Table

Below the invite form, a table lists all current and invited members:

| Column | What It Shows |
|--------|--------------|
| **MEMBER** | Avatar, full name, and email address |
| **ROLE IN PORTAL** | "OWNER" (green label), "Admin", or *"Invitation sent"* (for pending invites) |
| **JOB TITLE** | The member's assigned title, or "—" if none |

## Roles

- **Owner** — full access. There is one owner per account. No actions menu appears for the owner.
- **Admin** — access with administrative rights. Click the three-dot menu (⋯) on an admin's row to revoke their access. You'll be asked to confirm before it takes effect.
- **Invitation sent** — the member has been invited but hasn't accepted yet. Click the three-dot menu (⋯) to:
  - **Edit job title** — update the title before the member accepts
  - **Delete invitation** — cancel the pending invite

## Revoking Access

To remove an admin's access:

1. Find the member in the table
2. Click the three-dot menu (⋯) on their row
3. Select **Revoke admin**
4. Confirm when asked

The member loses access immediately. A confirmation message appears.
